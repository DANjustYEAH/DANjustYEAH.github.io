About

I'm Daniel Radford, Digital Media student at the University of the West of England.

Alongside my studies, I work at Nymbl creating virtual reality experiences using Unreal Engine and the Oculus Rift, HTC Vive and Samsung Gear.

I enjoy creating audio visualisers and working on live visuals for Crux Bristol.
Visual works include experimental musical arrangements by DXO accompanied by drastic 2D animation created in After Effects.

Currently, I run the 24/7 youtube radio station Cloud Chillout
